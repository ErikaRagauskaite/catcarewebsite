Our Services

At Cat Care Collective, we offer a range of services designed to make your cat feel comfortable, safe, and well cared for at home. Our services are tailored to meet the unique needs of each cat, ensuring a personalized experience every time. You can add additional pets for £5 per pet. Please note that prices may vary based on location.

Daily Drop-Ins

Perfect for regular care while you're away! We visit your home once or twice a day to feed, play, and care for your cat's daily needs for 30 minutes. Each session includes:

  • Feeding and fresh water
  • Litter box cleaning
  • Playtime and enrichment
  • Basic grooming (brushing, if they enjoy it)
  • Medication administration, if needed
  • Daily updates with photos
  • Price starting from £15 per visit

Extended Stay Visits

If your cat loves company, our extended stay visits are for them! We'll stay for a longer period (up to an hour) to provide more interaction and comfort, including:

  • All the same services as daily drop-ins
  • Outdoor time (leash-guided strolls, if comfortable)
  • Home check-ins (watering plants, bringing in mail)
  • Price starting from £20 per visit

Overnight Stays

For cats who need extra care and attention, we offer overnight stays in your home. We'll be there from evening to morning to provide companionship, care, and peace of mind, including:

  • All the same services as daily drop-ins and extended stay visits
  • Evening and morning feeding
  • Bedtime cuddles and playtime
  • Home security checks
  • Price starting from £50 per night

Special Care for Senior Cats or Cats with Health Needs

If your cat has health requirements, we offer attentive care to meet their specific needs, which may include:

  • Medication administration
  • Special diet preparation
  • Assistance with mobility or grooming
  • Regular health monitoring
  • Price starting from £20 per visit
